# CBN Cybersecurity Framework Checklist

A practical compliance checklist for the Central Bank of Nigeria's Risk-Based Cybersecurity Framework, mandatory for commercial banks, merchant banks, non-interest banks, and Payment Service Banks (PSBs) in Nigeria since 1 July 2024.

## Why This Exists

The CBN framework is not optional guidance. It replaced the 2018 guidelines and is actively enforced through annual supervisory reviews, risk-based examinations, industry-wide compliance audits, and spot checks. Yet many banks and PSPs are still treating it as a document to file away rather than a living compliance obligation.

This toolkit breaks the framework down into practical, checkable items so compliance, risk, and IT teams can track real readiness, not just awareness.

## Who This Is For

- Commercial banks, merchant banks, and non-interest banks in Nigeria
- Payment Service Banks (PSBs) and Payment Service Providers
- GRC and compliance consultants supporting financial institutions
- Internal audit teams preparing for CBN supervisory review

## Important Note

This framework explicitly references the Banks and Other Financial Institutions Act (BOFIA) 2020 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023. Your cybersecurity, banking regulatory, and data protection obligations are not three separate silos, they are interconnected under this framework.
